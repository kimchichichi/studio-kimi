about

Clarinetist and freelancer web designer, I create captivating websites tailored specifically for fellow musicians. In the wake of the pandemic, as the music world came to a standstill, the absence of live performances unexpectedly opened a gateway to a new creative realm. Inspired by the need to stay connected despite physical distances, it sparked my interest in coding, transforming it into a fulfilling hobby. With a background in music, I bring a unique understanding of the industry to my web design projects, ensuring that each site reflects the artist's essence. Combining creativity and technical expertise, I strive to enhance the online presence of musicians, helping them connect with the world in the digital pressence.
